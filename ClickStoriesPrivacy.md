# Click Stories Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2026

Click Stories respects your privacy. This app is designed to work without collecting personal information or transmitting your data to external servers.

## Information We Collect

Click Stories does not require account creation and does not collect personal information.

The app uses the following data locally on your device:

- **Location Data** — Used only to store where photos and videos were taken from.
- **Photos, videos** — Images and videos you choose to capture for notes.
- **Text Notes** — Notes you create for chapters.

All book/chapter/note/photo/video data is stored on your device.

## How Location Is Used

Location access is requested only while using the app.  
Location data is used solely to record photos and videos were taken from.

Click Stories does not track your location in the background after the app is closed.

## Data Storage

All recorded photos, videos and notes are stored locally on your device.  
Click Stories does not upload, transmit, or share your data.

## Data Sharing

Click Stories does not:

- Sell data  
- Share data with third parties  
- Use advertising networks  
- Use third-party analytics services  

## Third-Party Services

Click Stories uses Apple system frameworks (such as MapKit and CoreLocation) to provide mapping and GPS functionality. No third-party tracking SDKs are included.

## Children’s Privacy

Click Stories does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
